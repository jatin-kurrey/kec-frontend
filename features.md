# Admin & Management Feature Proposals (KEC)

These are advanced feature ideas designed specifically to empower the college administration, making the workflow highly automated and efficient.

## 💼 1. Built-in CRM (Lead & Admission Management)
* **Call & Follow-up Tracker:** Add internal notes next to every student application profile (e.g., *"Called on 12th Aug, parent asked to call back on Sunday"*). Include a calendar date picker for scheduling the next follow-up.
* **Bulk Communication (WhatsApp/Email):** Allow admins to select 50-100 students from the grid and shoot bulk WhatsApp messages or Emails in a single click (e.g., *"Admit cards are released!"* or *"Super 40 deadline is tomorrow"*).
* **UTM & Lead Source Tracking:** The dashboard will display exact traffic sources—detailing whether an applicant came from Facebook Ads, Google Ads Search, or a Direct Referral—allowing management to optimize the marketing budget.

## 🛡️ 2. Maker-Checker System (Approval Workflows)
* **Draft & Approve System:** When a junior staff member creates a new "Notice" or "Press Release," it will save in a `Draft` mode instead of going live instantly. The Principal or Super Admin can view it in the panel and click "Approve & Publish."
* **Activity Audit Logs:** A security table that permanently logs system activity: "Who did what, and when." (e.g., *"Admin Neha updated the Mechanical placement rate at 3:00 PM on 15th Aug"*). Essential for accountability.

## 📂 3. Document Verification Portal
* **Digital Uploading:** Allow students to upload their 10th/12th marksheets, Aadhar cards, or migration certificates while filling out their specific forms. 
* **Verification Queue:** A side-by-side dashboard view where admins can inspect the uploaded documents. An admin can click "Verified" to approve the profile, or "Reject (Blurry Image)" which triggers an auto-email instructing the student to re-upload.

## 💸 4. Payment Tracking & Fee Collection
* **Integrated Checkouts:** For premium forms (like the Super-40 or specific application fees), integrate Razorpay/PayTM APIs.
* **Status Dashboard:** Display Live statuses like `Fee Paid` or `Fee Pending`. Admins can select users with pending statuses and send a "Payment Reminder" auto-generated link instantly.

## 🎨 5. Homepage & UI Control Engine
* **Section Reordering:** During peak admission seasons, an admin can click a button to push the "Admission Banner" or "Placement Statistics" to the absolute top of the website without needing a developer to change React code.
* **Dynamic Gallery/Album Manager:** Admins can create bulk folders (e.g., "TechFest 2025" or "Convocation 2024") in the panel, drop 30 photos into it, and have them immediately populate on the public Gallery page categorized and styled perfectly.
